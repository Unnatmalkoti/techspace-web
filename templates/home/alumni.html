ALUMS RESEARCH MONETARY SCHOLARSHIP (ARMS)

This scholarship will be awarded to students for completing a project under Techspace. Form will be up in December and shortlisted Fellows list will be displayed by April. Fellowship will start in month of June. The project timeline extends till September and final submission will be done before InfoX. These projects will be showcased in infoxpression. The successful students will be known as ARMS fellow.

For early signup, submit the ARMS Fellowship Interest Form.
